Frequently Asked Questions

  • Search the Service: Identify the app or website where you need a temporary number.
  • Select Server: Choose a server offering your desired service.
  • Pick Country: Click the country button to process a number in that locale.
  • Send SMS: Send the code request—if it doesn’t appear right away, retry shortly.
  • Click Deposit: Start from your account dashboard.
  • Choose UPI or TRX: UPI gives a QR (valid 30 min); TRX works similarly.
  • Complete Payment: Within 30 min for UPI, or as instructed for TRX.
  • Need Help? Hit Help & Support for any payment errors or timeouts.
  • Access History: From main or profile menu.
  • View Last Week: See all deposits and orders.
  • Detailed Filters: Toggle between deposits & orders for specifics.
  • QR Validity: Must pay within 30 min.
  • Still Stuck? Contact Support via Help button.
  • Verify TX: Ensure it completed on-chain.
  • Help? Reach out via Support.
  • Auto‑Refund: After 20 min if no SMS received.
  • Note: No refund if SMS has already come through.
