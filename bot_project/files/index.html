FAQ

  • Search the Application/Website: Begin by identifying the service for which you need to buy a number or account.
  • Click the App Button: If it’s the same, click the app button.
  • Choose a Server: Pick one based on your preference.
  • Select a Country: Choose by pricing. Click to process your number.
  • Send SMS: Send the code request. If it doesn’t appear immediately, retry later.
  • Click Deposit: From your account, click the Deposit button.
  • Choose Payment Method: Select TRX (TRC20) or UPI.
  • UPI: QR code valid for 30 min—complete payment within that time.
  • TRX: Follow similar steps to UPI.
  • Issues? Click Help & Support for assistance.
  • Access History: From main or profile menu.
  • View Last Week: See all deposits and orders.
  • Detailed Filters: Toggle between deposits & orders.
  • Validate QR Code: Pay within the 30‑minute validity.
  • Contact Support: Use Help & Support if it persists.
  • Verify Transaction: Ensure it’s completed correctly.
  • Contact Support: If it continues, reach out.
  • Retry Later: New numbers are added regularly.
  • Automatic Refund: Wait 20 min for auto‑refund if no SMS.
  • Note: No refund if SMS has already been received.
